Role:
You are a senior mobile MVP engineer. Simplify the Trends screen to reduce cognitive load and improve trust. Do not add new features.
Context
The current Trends screen tries to infer causes (e.g., “you feel better when…”).
This creates:
low trust (data is limited)
high cognitive load
mismatch with the rest of the app (which is simple and directive)
We want Trends to be:
observational (not interpretive)
minimal
easy to read in low-energy states
GOAL
Transform Trends into a simple reflection layer:
Show what seems to help (1 line)
Show what seems to worsen symptoms (1 line)
Show one next step (1 line)
No more than that.
PART 1 — UPDATE ANALYZE FUNCTION OUTPUT
In analyze():
1. Remove causal language
Replace phrases like:
“You seem to feel better when…”
“X may be linked to…”
With observational phrasing only:
Use patterns like:
“On days with higher hydration, symptoms were lower.”
“Short sleep days have coincided with higher fatigue.”
“Days without compression have coincided with higher dizziness.”
Do NOT imply cause.
2. Limit output to ONE item per section
Change:
helps: helps.slice(0, 2),
worsens: worsens.slice(0, 2),
To:
helps: helps.slice(0, 1),
worsens: worsens.slice(0, 1),
3. Simplify "tryNext"
Replace all “Try…” phrasing.
Use:
tryNext.push("Next step: prioritize fluids earlier in the day.");
Pattern:
Always start with “Next step:”
Keep it short
Only ONE item
PART 2 — SIMPLIFY EMPTY STATE
Replace current long text:
Life with these symptoms is unpredictable...
With:
Title:
"You’re building your pattern"
Body:
"A few days of check-ins will help this become clearer."
PART 3 — REMOVE GENERIC “FOCUS” SECTION
Delete this entire block:
<View style={styles.focusCard}>
  ...
</View>
This section is not personalized and adds noise.
PART 4 — KEEP STRUCTURE SIMPLE
Final UI structure when data exists:
Card 1:
What seems to help
→ show only 1 line or “Not enough data yet”
Card 2:
What seems to make symptoms worse
→ show only 1 line or “Not enough data yet”
Card 3 (optional):
Next step
→ show 1 short line (only if available)
PART 5 — DO NOT CHANGE
Do NOT change export feature
Do NOT change data structure
Do NOT add charts or graphs
Do NOT add new metrics
Do NOT increase text length
ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA
Each section shows max 1 line
No causal language (“because”, “leads to”, “causes”)
Language is observational and simple
“Next step” starts with “Next step:”
Empty state is short and calm
No extra sections or noise
GOAL
User should feel:
“I understand my patterns a bit better — without needing to think.”
