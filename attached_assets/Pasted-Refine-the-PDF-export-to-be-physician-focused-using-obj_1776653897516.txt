Refine the PDF export to be physician-focused using objective numeric data and minimal language.

GOAL:
Create a clean, clinically useful report focused on vitals, orthostatic patterns, hydration, and frequency-based summaries.
Avoid non-validated scoring systems and avoid interpretation.

-----------------------------------
1. HEADER
-----------------------------------

Include:
- Norovia
- Export date
- Date range

-----------------------------------
2. LOGGING SUMMARY
-----------------------------------

Show:
- Days logged
- Symptom check-ins
- Vitals entries

-----------------------------------
3. VITALS SUMMARY
-----------------------------------

Blood pressure:
- Mean systolic BP
- Mean diastolic BP

Heart rate:
- Mean HR
- Min HR
- Max HR

-----------------------------------
4. ORTHOSTATIC SUMMARY (if data available)
-----------------------------------

If both seated and standing readings exist:

Show:
- Mean HR (seated)
- Mean HR (standing)
- Mean ΔHR (standing – seated)

Do not interpret values.

-----------------------------------
5. HR DISTRIBUTION
-----------------------------------

Show:
- Percentage of HR readings ≥ 100 bpm

-----------------------------------
6. HYDRATION AND SALT
-----------------------------------

Hydration:
- Mean daily fluid intake (L)
- Days with ≥ 2L intake

Salt:
- Days with added salt
- Days without added salt
- Days with salt supplements (if tracked)

-----------------------------------
7. VITALS TABLE
-----------------------------------

Columns:
- Date
- Time (if available)
- Systolic BP
- Diastolic BP
- Heart rate
- Context (seated / standing / other)

-----------------------------------
8. GRAPHS (optional)
-----------------------------------

If simple to implement:
- Add a basic line graph for HR over time
- Add a basic line graph for BP over time

Keep design minimal and neutral.

-----------------------------------
9. FOOTER
-----------------------------------

Add:
“This is self-reported tracking data from Norovia and is not a medical diagnosis.”

-----------------------------------
10. RULES
-----------------------------------

- No interpretation
- No diagnostic language
- No symptom scores
- No sleep score
- No recommendations
- Keep everything numeric, neutral, and clean
